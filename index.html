About Me

Hello world! My name is Paul Jerome Hernandez. But most call me PJ for short. I was born and raised in the Philippines until the age of 11. Since then, I have lived in Indonesia, Singapore, and the United States.

I'm currently based in Los Angeles studying Computer Science at the University of Southern California. I love the idea of improving aspects of life through technology, no matter how small in scale. Because of my global exposure, I'm also constantly on the move looking for things to inspire me. I hope to use my technical skills to help businesses grow and reach their full potential anywhere in the world.

Projects

USC's The Supper Club

A few of my college friends and I founded USC's very first food club. Our mission is to create an inclusive group of like-minded people to explore LA's food scene. thesupperclubsc.com serves as a platform for current members to engage with the club's mission and activities. Member can see upcoming events, sign up for activities, and engage with our club. The site was built with Paper Kit 2.0, a Bootstrap UI Kit that gave the frontend a 'foodie' club feel. Check out my GitHub for the code.



Track Changes

My first attempt on making a mobile app was a group effort called Track Changes, a music-streaming app with more interactive social features inspired by apps like Instagram. Our team wanted to focus on building a social community around today's music streaming apps. A user can build their social profule, follow other users, follow music-related content, and share music content with other users - these are the few features we saw were acking in apps like Apple Music and Spotify.
