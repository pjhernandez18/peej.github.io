About Me

I'm currently based in Los Angeles studying Computer Science at the University of Southern California. I love the idea of developing technology products that improve any aspect of life, no matter how small in scale. I'm currently open to both Software Engineering positions that will help me enhance my technical repertoire and Product Manager positions that will help me gain experience building and shipping technology products at scale.

Projects

USC's The Supper Club

A few of my college friends and I founded USC's very first food club. Our mission is to create an inclusive group of USC foodies to explore LA's food scene. thesupperclubsc.com serves as a platform for current members to engage with the club's mission and activities. Member can see upcoming events, sign up for activities, and engage with our club. The site was built with HTML/CSS/Bootstrap. Check out my GitHub for the code.



Token

Token a music-streaming app with an emphasis building a social platform inspired by apps like Instagram and Facebook. My team and I wanted to focus on building a social community, while also maintaining the same features of today's music streaming apps like Apple Music and Spotify. For development, we used the Spotify Web API to allow registered Spotify users to be authenticated and build their social profile. Users will be able to follow other users and engage with today's hottest music content.
