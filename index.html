Projects

USC's The Supper Club (Current)

A few of my college friends and I founded USC's very first food scene exploration club. Our mission is to foster open-minded foodies to explore LA's rich food scene. thesupperclubsc.com serves as a platform for current members to engage with the club's mission and activities. Members can sign up for upcoming events, give restaurant recommendations, and engage with club activities. The current site is built on core HTML/CSS/Bootstrap.


Currently in the works in revamping the website for the 2019-20 USC semester. I will be refactoring the frontend/UI using ReactJS/Gatsby, implementing a CMS to easily manage site content for our club members, and host it on Heroku.



Token

Token is an iOS app that allows users to share their travel experiences with other friends in the form of "Tokens". Each trip has a Token associated with it that contains photos, videos, and highlights from your trip. A user can either choose to share that Token with other friends or keep it for themselves to look back on. Our team realized that there isn't really an easy way to share travel experiences in way that fully encapsualtes the essence of your trip. So, our team wanted to make an app that gives users the freedom document and share their Tokens, backed by the infastructure of a social media application.
